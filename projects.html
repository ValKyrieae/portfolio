Digital Forensics Capstone

Growth & Reflection

When I started this journey, I honestly just wanted to understand how things work. I didn’t know how to script, I wasn’t confident presenting, and I definitely didn’t know what tools like Autopsy or Wireshark really did. Now, I’ve built forensic reports, presented capstone findings in a mock court setting, and configured real-world security settings as an ISSO.

Each project here represents a challenge I stepped into and figured out — sometimes the hard way — but that’s what makes it real. I’m proud of how far I’ve come and excited about where I’m heading.

If you want to talk about learning curves, troubleshooting nightmares, or nerding out over packet captures — I’m your person.
